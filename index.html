About Us

Kalaithai Silambam Academy is dedicated to teaching the ancient art of Silambam, a traditional Indian martial art originating from Tamil Nadu. We combine traditional values with modern training methods to make this ancient discipline accessible and relevant for today's practitioners.

Our weekend classes focus on physical fitness, mental discipline, and cultural heritage, creating well-rounded individuals who excel both in martial arts and in life. Perfect for students and working professionals.
